Bryan Allison, NASCAR Sprint Cup Series driver, was arrested on Sunday for a felony charge of lewd act during an incident at a Daytona 500 garage.

The charge carries a sentence of five to 20 years in prison and a $250,000 fine and is punishable by up to six months in jail.

The incident occurred while Allison was at the track with his wife, Nicole, at the time.

"I know there are a lot of people out there who don't like the way he handles things," Allison said. "I've had some very good friends that are saying, 'What the f--- is going on here? It's not right.'"

The incident was reported to the Daytona International Speedway on Sunday afternoon and was reported to the police at 10:25 p.m.

A ticket was issued for the charge but there were no charges filed against Allison.
