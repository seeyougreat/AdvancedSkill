Two killed and scores injured in Germany as car ploughs into crowd at Christmas market - The Guardian

A car ploughs into a crowd near a Christmas market in Germany - The Guardian

A man was killed and at least three wounded in what was described as an "ambush" in the southern German city of Schleswig-Holstein, officials said.

The man was killed as his car was ploughing through the crowds at the Christmas market in the southeastern city of Schleswig-Holstein, Interior Minister Thomas de Maiziere told reporters.

The man was in his 40s and a baby was in the back seat, he added.

He was shot several times and was taken to a hospital in Berlin, where he later died.
