Village People to play at Trump inaugural rally - Axios

Trump, who is scheduled to be sworn in at 1 p.m., said he is "not going to speculate" on what he will do next.

"I will tell you right now, I'm not going to speculate, and I'm going to do what is right for the country," he said.

He also promised to build a wall on the southern border to keep out immigrants, saying he will build it "to stop illegal immigration."

He tweeted that he will leave "the United States" and will not be returning to "the United States to raise my children."

He also expressed his support for the Republican nominee, saying he had not yet decided who he would vote for.

"I'm proud to work for him and I'm proud to vote for him," he said.
